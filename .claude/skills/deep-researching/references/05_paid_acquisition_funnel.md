## 1. Facebook/Meta Ads Strategy (Creative-First Approach)

**IMPORTANT:** Interest-based targeting and audience stacking are dead. Meta's algorithm now handles targeting through broad parameters (location, age, gender). The AD CREATIVE is what "targets" by attracting and filtering the right audience. All recommendations should focus on creative strategy, not targeting.

### Cold Traffic Creative (Top of Funnel)
- What creative formats work for cold audiences in this industry? (static vs video vs carousel vs UGC-style)
- What hooks stop the scroll for people who don't know this brand yet?
- What's the core message that qualifies/disqualifies viewers in the first 3 seconds?
- Based on Chapter 1 psychology, what emotional trigger leads the ad?
- Video structure if applicable: hook -> problem -> solution -> CTA (with timing)
- Example ad concepts (2-3 complete creative ideas with hook + body + CTA)

### Warm Traffic Creative (Middle of Funnel)
- What creative works for people who've engaged but not converted?
- How does messaging shift from awareness to consideration?
- Testimonial/case study approaches that build trust
- Educational content that addresses Chapter 1 objections
- Retargeting creative angles (what to say to someone who visited but didn't book)

### Hot Traffic Creative (Bottom of Funnel)
- What creative closes? Direct response angles for ready-to-buy audiences
- Urgency/scarcity approaches appropriate for this audience
- Offer-focused creative vs trust-focused creative
- How to handle final objections in ad creative

### Hook Library
- Based on Chapter 1 pains, desires, and obstacles, provide 7-10 specific hooks
- For each hook: the angle, why it works for this psychology, example opening line
- Rank hooks by expected effectiveness for this audience

### Creative Production Requirements
- Video: talking head vs lifestyle vs testimonial vs educational - which works?
- Ideal video length by funnel stage
- Production value expectations (polished vs raw/authentic)
- Visual aesthetic direction (colors, style, energy level)
- UGC requirements if applicable

### Platform/Placement Notes
- Instagram vs Facebook weighting for this demographic
- Any format considerations for Reels vs Feed vs Stories
