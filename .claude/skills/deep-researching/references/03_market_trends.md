# Chapter 3: Market Trends & Cultural Shifts

**MISSION:** Identify the macro and micro trends shaping the industry/market described in the audience brief. Understand the cultural moment we're marketing into.

**RESEARCH AREAS:**

## 1. Macro Industry Trends

- Search for current trends in the specific industry/category from the audience brief
- Use search terms like: "[industry] trends 2025", "[category] trends 2026", "[market] evolution"
- Identify major shifts happening in the industry (technology, consumer behavior, innovation, regulation, etc.)
- Look for emerging trends, new approaches, or backlash against old methods
- Find data, statistics, or industry reports when available
- Include representative examples or statistics when available

## 2. Cultural Dynamics & Regional Factors

- If a specific location is mentioned in the audience brief, research local culture and preferences
- What defines the cultural norms or standards in that location for this category?
- Search for local publications, lifestyle magazines, business journals relevant to the area
- Identify any regional differences from broader national/international trends
- If no specific location, focus on broader cultural shifts affecting this audience demographic

## 3. Economic Behavior & Spending Patterns

- How is the target demographic (from audience brief) approaching spending in this category?
- Search for: "[demographic] spending trends", "[income level] [category] spending", "[industry] market trends"
- Are they prioritizing quality over price? Experience over cost? Convenience? Sustainability?
- Any shifts in what they value (expertise, outcomes, speed, ethics, etc.)?
- Consider economic factors (inflation, recession fears, consumer confidence) affecting this demographic

## 4. Search Behavior & Keyword Trends

- Identify trending search terms this audience actually uses when looking for solutions in this category
- Look for evidence of what they're searching for (Google Trends data if available, SEO tools, social media hashtags)
- What language indicates they're part of this specific demographic vs. the mass market?
- What pain points or solution keywords are trending?
- What terminology shift is happening (old terms falling out of favor, new terms rising)?

## Search Strategy

Based on the audience brief, construct searches like:
- "[industry/category] trends 2025" OR "2026"
- "[industry] market analysis" + 2025
- "[demographic descriptor] + [category] + behavior" OR "preferences"
- "[location if provided]" + "[category]" + "trends" OR "culture"
- "[industry] + spending trends" OR "market size"
- Google Trends for category keywords
- Industry-specific publications, reports, and analysts
- Social media hashtag analysis for trending topics
- "[category] statistics 2025" OR "market report"

**Adapt by industry type:**
- B2B SaaS: Search for "enterprise software trends", Gartner reports, analyst opinions
- Consumer Products: Search for consumer behavior studies, retail trends, e-commerce shifts
- Professional Services: Industry association reports, regulatory changes, market dynamics
- Local Services: Local market studies, demographic shifts in the area, competitive density

## Important Instructions

- Focus on CURRENT trends, not outdated information
- Distinguish between national/global trends and location-specific factors (if applicable)
- Provide evidence for trends when possible (cite sources, statistics, or observed patterns)
- If you find conflicting trends, note them both and explain the nuance
- Make trends ACTIONABLE - explain why each trend matters for the marketing strategy
- Connect trends back to the specific audience described in the brief

**OUTPUT GOAL:** 4-5 pages of trend analysis that provides deep contextual awareness of the current market moment, cultural dynamics (if location-specific), economic factors, and how the target audience is evolving in their approach to this category.
