# Lead Magnet Delivery Flow

**2 emails | Immediate + Day 1-2 | NO selling**

## Purpose

Deliver what was promised while subscriber interest is highest. This is NOT the time to sell — it's the time to build trust by following through immediately.

---

## Required Inputs

Before writing, gather:

| Input | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Lead magnet name | What they signed up for |
| Lead magnet type | PDF, video, checklist, guide, etc. |
| Lead magnet description | Brief summary of what's inside |
| Quick-win tip | One specific tip from the resource |
| Sender name | Who signs the emails |

---

## Email 1: Delivery (Immediate)

**Goal:** Deliver the asset and set expectations

### Must Include:
1. Thank them for signing up (brief, not gushy)
2. Deliver the asset with a clear, prominent link: `[DOWNLOAD LINK PLACEHOLDER]`
3. Brief explanation of how to use it or what to look for first
4. Set expectations: "Over the next few days, I'll share [what's coming]..."

### Tone
Warm, direct, focused

### Subject Line Examples
- "Here's your {Lead Magnet Name}"
- "Your free {Lead Magnet Type} is inside"
- "{Lead Magnet Name} — download now"

### Template

```
[Hook — get right to it]

You asked for {Lead Magnet Name}. Here it is:

[DOWNLOAD LINK PLACEHOLDER]

[1-2 sentences on how to use it or what to look for first]

[Set expectations for upcoming emails]

{Sender Name}
```

---

## Email 2: Consumption Nudge (1-2 Days Later)

**Goal:** Get them to actually USE the resource

### Must Include:
1. Reminder about the asset (include the link again): `[DOWNLOAD LINK PLACEHOLDER]`
2. Share one quick-win tip from the resource
3. Engagement prompt: Encourage a reply (e.g., "Hit reply and tell me which tip you tried first")

### Tone
Helpful, encouraging

### Subject Line Examples
- "Did you see this part?"
- "Quick tip from your {Lead Magnet Name}"
- "The one thing most people miss in the {Lead Magnet Type}"

### Template

```
[Hook — reference the resource]

Quick reminder: your {Lead Magnet Name} is waiting.

[DOWNLOAD LINK PLACEHOLDER]

Here's one tip that makes a big difference:

[Quick-win tip — specific, actionable]

[Engagement prompt — ask them to reply]

{Sender Name}
```

---

## Key Rules

1. **Email 1 MUST be instant** — within minutes of opt-in
2. **Re-link the lead magnet in Email 2** — they probably haven't downloaded yet
3. **Keep each email under 200 words** — short and focused
4. **Do NOT include:**
   - Sales pitch or product mention
   - Brand history or origin story
   - Multiple topics or asks

---

## Business Type Adaptations

| Business Type | Lead Magnet Example | Email 2 Angle |
|---------------|---------------------|---------------|
| Coaches/Consultants | PDF guide, checklist | "Did you try the exercise on page 2? Here's what most people get wrong..." |
| Local Services | Maintenance checklist, hiring guide | Bonus tip related to seasonal concerns |
| Course Creators | Mini-training video, sample chapter | Link to companion worksheet or insider tip |
| Professional Services | Compliance guide, planning checklist | "Here's what to prepare before your consultation" |
