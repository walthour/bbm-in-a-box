# Template 4: Lead-Gen / Services / Medical

## Best For
- Services requiring consultation (medical, legal, B2B)
- Goal is form fills or calls, not immediate purchase
- Local businesses
- Professional services

## Structure

### 1. Hero "Who This Is For" + Outcome
- **Headline**: Target + outcome ("Knee pain relief without surgery")
- **Subheadline**: Clarify approach, location if relevant
- Primary CTA: "Request consultation," "Check eligibility," "Get a quote"

### 2. Credibility Snapshot
- 3-5 trust elements:
  - Years in practice
  - Patients/clients served
  - Certifications, awards
  - Review score
- 1-2 small patient/client quotes

### 3. How It Works (Steps)
- 3-4 steps from now to outcome
  - Example: "1. Schedule → 2. Assessment → 3. Treatment plan"
- Small icons next to each step

### 4. Conditions/Problems We Help With
- Bulleted list or grid, grouped by category
- Short line reinforcing specialization

### 5. Deeper Proof & Authority
- Longer testimonial or case example
- Before/after results
- Logos: insurers accepted, affiliations, press mentions

### 6. Risk & Logistics
- Address price expectations, insurance, timing
- Location, safety, qualified staff
- Micro-FAQ (4-6 questions)

### 7. Form Block
- Short form above the fold (desktop)
- Repeated lower down after proof
- Very clear "what happens after you submit" copy
- Minimal fields (name, email, phone, brief qualifier)

## Key Success Factors
- Lead with credibility early
- Make the process crystal clear
- Multiple form placements
- Manage expectations on response time
