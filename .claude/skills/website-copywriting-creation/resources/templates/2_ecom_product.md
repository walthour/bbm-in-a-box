# Template 2: Direct-to-Product Ecom

## Best For
- Single-product campaigns from Meta, TikTok, YouTube
- Cold paid traffic
- Physical or digital products

## Structure

### 1. Hero Product Section
- Product image or lifestyle shot
- **Headline**: Main outcome or differentiator
- Price display
- 3-5 key value prop bullets
- Primary "Add to cart / Buy now" CTA

### 2. "Why This Works" Strip
- Short paragraph: core mechanism
- 3 icons with micro-copy ("Fast," "Clinically tested," "Eco-friendly")

### 3. Social Proof Block
- Star rating summary
- 3-5 highlighted reviews with faces
- Badges: press, certifications, awards

### 4. Detailed Benefits Section
- 3-6 benefit modules
- Each: image + headline + 1-2 lines
- Zig-zag or grid layout for scanning

### 5. "What's Inside / Specs"
- Ingredients, materials, sizing, technical details
- Table or bullet format
- Guarantees, standards (hypoallergenic, FDA-registered, etc.)

### 6. Objections & Risk Reversal
- FAQ: delivery, returns, side effects, fit
- Explicit guarantee or return policy
- Repeated CTA

### 7. Final Urgency Strip
- Scarcity/urgency if legitimate (limited run, seasonal)
- "Choose your bundle" options
- Final CTA
