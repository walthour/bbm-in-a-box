# Template 5: Pre-Sell Advertorial / Listicle

## Best For
- Bridge from paid social to product page
- Educating or reframing before the ask
- Overcoming skepticism with "content"
- Ecom products requiring explanation

## Structure

### 1. "Editorial" Hero
- **Headline**: Content-style format
  - "7 Reasons Women Over 40 Are Switching to…"
  - "The Weird Trick Dermatologists Don't Want You to Know"
- **Byline**: Sponsored by [Brand] (compliance-visible)

### 2. Context/Story Intro
- 2-5 short paragraphs setting up problem
- Establish reader identity
- Soft mention of brand/product as the discovery

### 3. Listicle or Sections
- 5-9 list items
- Each ties a pain point to a product benefit
- Format per item:
  - Small image
  - Bolded first phrase
  - 2-4 lines explanation

### 4. Embedded Proof
- Screenshots of reviews
- Small before/after images
- Expert quote or endorsement
- Callouts: "clinically tested," "doctor-recommended" (only if accurate)

### 5. Transition to "The Solution"
- Short section: why this solution beats alternatives
- Strong visual of product bundle

### 6. Product CTA Block
- Mini product section:
  - Price
  - Options/bundles
  - Key benefits summary
- "Shop now" button
- Optional urgency (limited discount, free shipping)

## Tone
Balance editorial feel with sales intent. Must still be transparent about sponsorship but feel like helpful content, not a hard pitch.
